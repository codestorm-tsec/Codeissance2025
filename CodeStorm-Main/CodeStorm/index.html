Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your adventure

Who can participate?

The hackathon is open for Students from all over India. Undergraduate Engineering Students of all branches are eligible to participate.

How much will it cost?

Registration for Codeissance 2025 is completely free.

How can I apply?

The registration can be done by simply clicking the Unstop button above and filling up the form, where you will mention you and your team details.

What are the prizes to be won?

Winners will receive cash prizes up to ₹20,000 from each domain, mentorship opportunities with industry leaders. Plus, bragging rights as the ultimate Codeissance champions!

What if I do not know how to code?

No worries, Interest in learning and working with technology is much more important than your current experience level. Beginners are also invited.

Can I attend the Hackathon remotely?

The hackathon will be conducted entirely in offline mode.
